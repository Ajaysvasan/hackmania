Daily Workout Challenge

Full Body Blast

Get ready for an intense full-body workout that will challenge your strength and endurance!

  • 20 Jumping Jacks
  • 15 Push-ups
  • 30 Squats
  • 20 Mountain Climbers
  • 1-minute Plank
  • 15 Burpees
  • 20 Lunges (10 each leg)
  • 30-second Wall Sit

Remember: Every rep brings you closer to your goals. Stay strong and push through!
